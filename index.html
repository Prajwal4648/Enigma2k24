About the event

The IT forum events offer a diverse range of activities for BCA students. Participants engage in coding challenges to showcase their programming prowess and in web design competitions to demonstrate their creative and technical skills. Ice-breaking activities are organized to foster camaraderie among participants. Discussions and workshops on IT management provide insights into effective resource and project management. Fun games like dum charades and outdoor gaming offer a break from technology and encourage teamwork and physical activity.

Participants also embark on a digital treasure hunt using laptops and explore the latest technologies at the tech exhibition. They showcase their artistic abilities through designing on canvas software and compete in an IT quiz to test their knowledge. Photography and videography events allow participants to capture moments and unleash their storytelling skills. Mobile gaming competitions and MAD ADS challenges add excitement and creativity to the event lineup. Overall, the IT forum events provide a platform for BCA students to learn, collaborate, and showcase their talents in various facets of information technology.

About Us

DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS

The Department of COMPUTER APPLICATION at Sacred Heart College Madanthyar is dedicated to providing its students with a comprehensive education in computer applications and information technology. With a focus on hands-on learning, the department strives to equip students with the technical skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in the rapidly evolving world of technology.

As part of this mission, the department is proud to announce the upcoming IT Fest, Enigma 2K24. This exciting event will be held on 23rd April 2024, and is organized entirely by the students of the BCA department. With 14 events and attractive prizes, ENIGMA 2024 promises to be an exciting showcase of the talent and creativity of the students of the BCA department.

Whether you're a student of the Computer Science department or simply interested in technology, ENIGMA 2024 is an event you won't want to miss. With opportunities to participate, learn and compete, this IT Fest is a perfect opportunity for anyone with an interest in technology to showcase their skills and connect with others who share their passion. So join us on 23rd April 2024 for ENIGMA 2024, an exciting and engaging IT Fest that promises to be a celebration of technology, creativity and the passion of the students of the BCA department at Sacred Heart College Madanthyar.
